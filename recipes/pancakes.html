Pancakes

Photo by Luke Pennystan on Unsplash

Description

Pancakes are my comfort / soul food. They are easily made - with just a few ingredients.

Pancakes pair well with maple syrup, compote or cinnamon sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup flour and 2 tsp baking powder in a large bowl. Add 2 tbsp white sugar and a pinch of salt. Now mix everything in the bowl.
  2. Pour in 1 cup milk, 2 tbsp vegetable oil and 1 large egg. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop about ¼ cup batter per pancake onto the frying pan; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry - 1 to 2 minutes. Flip; cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  5. Serve hot with all the toppings you like best.
